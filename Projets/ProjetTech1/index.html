Top Selling Guitarists artists of the month!

Barns Courtney is an English singer, songwriter and musician. His musical style is defined as Pop-Folk. With his drawling voice, Barns Courtney immerses us in the world of the golden age of British rock. His first album, "The Attractions of Youth", was released in 2017. His leitmotif: face the world and make you sweat as much as possible.

Archy Marshall, born August 24, 1994, is an English singer, songwriter and musician. He began music under a first stage name Zoo Kid and then continued from 2011 under the pseudonym King Krule. His music has been compared to those of Morrissey and Edwyn Collins. His style is considered dark wave, new wave and using Dub techniques.

Oliver Tree is a multi-talented artist: singer, producer and songwriter, all in an eclectic universe. Navigating both in psychedelic pop notes like with All I Got or in arpeggios interweaving pop and hipHop just as singular with Welcome To L.A, this artist hits hard!
